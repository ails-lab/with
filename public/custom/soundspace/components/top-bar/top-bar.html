Spot musical instruments

Have an ear for musical instruments? Use your talent to tag all the instruments you hear in the music archives of Europeana Sounds! There are so many historical music recordings in our archives, that it is impossible to describe all the instruments by ourselves. This is why we kindly ask your help to tag our beloved music collections with all the instruments that you can hear in a song. The music in Europeana Sounds ranges from beautiful folk songs from Greece to Scandinavian improvised space rock, all the way to sounds coming from Tibetian monasteries.
